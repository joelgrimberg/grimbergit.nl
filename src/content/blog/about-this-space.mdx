This space is where I write things down before I forget them.

Mostly patterns. The ones that keep showing up across teams and codebases, the ones that used to
look clever and now look expensive, the ones without a name yet that probably need one. A quality
practice that survives a refactor. A test suite that gets faster instead of slower. The specific
shape of an on-call rotation a team can actually keep up with.

Sometimes thoughts — ideas that are still forming, opinions I want to argue with in six months,
the paragraph that started as a Slack message and grew into something worth reading twice.

Occasionally notes. From a training I ran, a talk I gave, a project that ended well or ended
badly. The ones where writing it down is how I figure out what actually happened.

No cadence. When there's something worth writing, I'll write it. When there isn't, the last entry
is still here.
