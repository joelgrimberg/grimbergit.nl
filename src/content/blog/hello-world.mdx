Welcome. This site is where I collect the things I think out loud about — mostly platforms,
reliability, and the human bits of shipping software.

## Why now

I've been meaning to have my own corner of the internet again for years. The trigger, honestly,
was buying the domain: once the receipt landed, procrastinating became embarrassing.

## What to expect

- Short essays, when I have something to say.
- Field notes from consulting and training work (anonymised).
- The occasional talk write-up.

Toggle the theme in the header if the default is too calm — the _nerd_ mode swaps in the Nord
palette and drops a faded wallpaper behind everything, for readers who like to feel at home in a
terminal.
